Our Academy

A Community of Artists

Tandvikaa School of Dance & Performing Arts was born from a powerful belief: that dance has the power to transform lives. Founded in New Delhi, we've built a space where beginners discover their passion and experienced dancers perfect their craft.

Our academy is equipped with premium lighting, top-quality sound systems, and spacious floor-to-ceiling mirrors — the perfect environment for dance, fitness, and performance.

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To cultivate artistic excellence and personal growth through the transformative power of dance — making quality education accessible to all ages and abilities, while nurturing confidence, creativity, and community in every student.

🌟 Our Vision

To become Delhi's most celebrated dance academy — a beacon of artistic excellence that inspires generations of dancers and performing artists, where every student's story is beautifully told through movement.

Founder's Message

A Message from Kiranjeet

"Dance has always been more than just a hobby for me — it has been my passion, my escape, and my way of connecting with the world."

When I started Tandvikaa School of Dance, my dream was simple: to create a place where anyone, no matter their age or experience, could feel the same joy and freedom that dance has given me.

Together, let's celebrate movement, rhythm, and life — one step at a time.

Modern · Lyrical · Experimental

Classical · Traditional · Expressive
